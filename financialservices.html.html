Financial Services

Royal Hand Solutions, LLC offers a wide range of financial services for small businesses and start-ups. Below is the list of financial projects and financial maintenance services that can be provided.

Financial Projects:
  • Books Set-up - Initial creation of the chart of accounts and/or accounting system
  • Modify Books Set-Up - Modify chart of accounts and/or accounting system
  • Creation of Accounting Processes - Analysis of daily / weekly / monthly accounting tasks with creation of internal processes.
  • Gross Profit Analysis Project Planner - Custom spreadsheet to show costs and gross profit per project/job (used as an estimate / job planner).
  • Cash-Flow Management Set-Up - Spreadsheet set-up for cash flow management - shows estimated ins and out for 3 months.
  • End of Year Financials - General audit of annual books in preparation for tax filings.
  • Custom Accounting Projects - Any accounting project client requests other than those listed above.
Financial Maintenance:
  • Monthly Books - End of Month financials prepartion for business analysis. Cash or accrual accounting.
  • Accounting Training (Hourly) - Training of created processes to an internal team member.
  • Custom Accounting Maintenance - Any accounting maintenance tasks client requests other than those listed above.
